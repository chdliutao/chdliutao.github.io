Posted on 2019-10-23

部分linux命令记录

lab-03-01：

  • Find the mintzquotes.txt file somewhere inside the maze directory

  • From the mintzquotes.txt file, extract all lines containing the string “math” in the first 30 lines of the file, and append them to your output file.

  • Take the last 10 lines of mintzquotes.txt, sort them in alphabetical order, number the resulting lines, then append the result to your output file.

  • Output a list of directories in the top level of maze/one in reverse order, and append this list to your output file.

  • Find the total number of subdirectories (recursively, so including directories inside of directories) inside of maze/two and append this to your output file.

  • Find the total number of files (recursively) inside of maze with names end in the suffix ‘ix’, and append this to your output file.

  • Find the total number of subdirectories that are named “garamond” inside of maze and append this to your output file.

  • Find the total number of files (recursively) inside of maze with name containing at least a single character ‘a’ that occur at depth at most 2. Append this to your output file.

  • Recursively delete every directory whose name ends in the suffix ‘ux’. (Hint: xargs.)

  • Match phone #s in contact.html, e.g. (206) 685-2181.

  • Move the year from the end of the line to the beginning of the line.

  • Do this and also sort the movies by year
